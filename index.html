Dataset

These visualizations are based on the SFO Air Traffic Passenger Statistics dataset from DataSF. This dataset contains passenger counts from all passenger flights in and out of San Francisco International Airport between 2005 and 2019, broken down by several metrics including region, airline, terminal, and boarding area. The dataset includes 21,228 rows and 12 columns. I accessed this dataset on January 6th, 2020 and is published with the ODC Public Domain Dedication and License.
